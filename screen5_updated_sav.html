01 — Executive Overview
Campaign Vision
Savanna enters 2026 at a defining moment — the opportunity to claim new positioning territory with a bold, movement-driven platform. Laughterhythm ('From here, where to?') encapsulates a restless, forward-moving spirit that speaks directly to the modern Mexican consumer who refuses to stand still. This Comms Plan outlines the strategic framework to bring this new territory to life across ATL, BTL, and always-on channels.
"Laughterhythm" — More than a tagline. A rallying cry for those who are always moving forward. Active, ambitious, and refreshingly light.

02 — Consumer Intelligence
Target Audience Insights
Data synthesis from Heineken's AMER consumer panel and WPP's proprietary Mexico market research identifies the core Savanna growth segment:
  • Primary Segment: Active urban males, 21–35, Mexico City / Guadalajara / Monterrey — pace-setters in their social circles
  • Occasion Over-index: Post-activity refreshment (sport, work, night out) — beer as the reward for momentum
  • Light Beer Affinity: 68% of primary segment actively seeking lighter options without sacrificing taste or social credibility
  • Digital Behaviour: 84% daily social media — TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube primary platforms; sports content over-index
  • Cultural Insight: Strong pride in Mexican identity; brands that reflect authentic ambition resonate deeply
